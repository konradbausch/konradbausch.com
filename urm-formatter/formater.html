URM Formatter

URM is a tool used by the University of Tübingen to grade assignments.

The URM-Formatter simplifies the creation of URM Files for groups.

Everything happens localy on your computer, no data is sent to any server.

If you possess a configuration file, simply upload it.

Otherwise, specify the number of teams and press "New Config". Then, add students to these teams, and remember to download your configuration for future use. Next, allocate points to each team, input the assignment number, and set the maximum points.

Once completed, download the generated URM File.
